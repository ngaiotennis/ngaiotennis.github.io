Welcome to the Ngaio Tennis club, a community and family friendly club offering a wide range of tennis services for players of all levels and abilities including beginners, mid-week ladies, social players and 13 senior interclub teams competing successfully at all levels of competition.

Ngaio offer regular weekly club sessions, social events, a number of well-organised Junior and Senior tournaments, and fun-based activities aimed at improving general skills and fitness.

In recent years we have put a strong focus on nurturing and developing our Junior club members. We offer a variety of coaching programmes ranging from free coaching for novice junior members (designed to provide the skills and confidence to take a complete beginner through to interclub entry level) through to interclub and development squad based programmes designed to take players to the highest level.

For further information regarding the club see the seniors and juniors sections.

We look forward to welcoming you and your family and friends to many successful and fun-filled seasons at the Ngaio Tennis Club.
